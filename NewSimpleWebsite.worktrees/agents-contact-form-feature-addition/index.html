About Me

I'm a self taught full-stack developer and Mechanical Engineering student at TU/e in Eindhoven, with a strong focus on robotics, automation, and industrial systems. Over the past year I've built end-to-end projects ranging from PLC-based diagnostics tools in a factory setting, to web-integrated puzzle delivery systems for real users. Beyond engineering, I love to travel, hike and build things with smart people.
